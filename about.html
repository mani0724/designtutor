About DESIGN TUTOR

Teaching Design Strategy

Learn to create Human Centric Designs using the acquired knowledge, developed skills, suitable tools with a clear mind-set.

Are you one of the students who are in search of a mentor, who is willing to guide you in your design project?

Are you unable to start or progress in a design project?

Are you clueless about the tools that can be used to express your design effectively?

If you answer is “Yes”, well then you have landed at the right place. We will provide individual attention to help you solve the problem, from the convenience of your home at your preferred time. This web platform guides the students on where to find the requisite Architectural data followed by how to evaluate them and then to use them as Design cursors.

The Design professionals engaged in this venture, by the use of online tools, will assist the individual from the first line drawn till the final presentation of any project in the Design stream. These mentors will attempt to focus the thought process of the amateur designer to arrive at amicable solutions. They will be intellectually engaging and technically updated in the latest trends. On the other hand, the participating students will have a forum to offer their ideas to their own peers, thus sharing the acquired knowledge. Not to stop with this, this portal will act as a knowledge sharing platform, wherein the students can brainstorm the various solutions for a given problem.
