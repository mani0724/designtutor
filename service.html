What We Do

  • Home
  • Our Courses
01

Design Mentor

The Design mentors share their profound knowledge pertaining to a specific design. One to One interaction between the student and the Guide will direct the designer on achieving the intended project objective.


Focus will be on

Extracting the standard data using Self Analysis

Understanding the circulation pattern & zoning

Designing with cross-functional spaces

Substantiating and presenting in a coherent manner

02

Memory Book

The students will be assisted (Online/offline) in making a portfolio consisting of their past projects to help them fetch an internship/job in a reputed firm.


Focus will be on

Selecting the appropriate projects

Framing and composing the data into a portfolio




03

Frame of reference

The students will be guided through the thesis project which acts as a culminating exercise of the entire course curriculum.


Focus will be on

The Area of Research

Converting the hypothesis into a design format

Framing and composing the data into a Project Report



04

Speak Out!-

This forum brings together all the interested students to view and comment on another student’s design (Online), thus sharing their ideas and thoughts which might in turn help the designer involved.


Focus will be on

The Communication skills

Exploring new ideas as a team
